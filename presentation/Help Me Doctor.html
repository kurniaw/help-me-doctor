The Problem
Knowing where to go
shouldn't add to the emergency.
01

Symptom Overload

Patients describe symptoms in plain language but don't know which specialty, hospital, or urgency level applies — leading to wrong-department visits and wasted time.

02

Medical–Legal Complexity

Incidents like workplace injuries carry both medical and legal implications. Users rarely know to address both simultaneously, causing missed deadlines (e.g. MOM reporting).

03

Fragmented Information

Doctor directories, hospital specialties, legal obligations, and emergency contacts exist across dozens of sources — impossible to navigate under stress.

Help Me Doctor
The Solution
One input. Four agents.
The right answer, fast.

Help Me Doctor is a Singapore-focused triage platform. Users describe their situation in natural language — the system parses it, searches curated knowledge bases, and streams back a prioritised action plan.

Built on a LangGraph multi-agent pipeline with RAG over medical and legal databases, it handles three case types: Medical, Legal, and Dual — simultaneously when needed.

Urgency Detection

LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / CRITICAL routing from plain-language input

Specialist Matching

Named doctors, hospitals, and forensic specialists from Singapore databases

Dual-Pathway Coordination

Handles medical care and legal obligations simultaneously when both apply

Architecture
The 4-Agent LangGraph Pipeline
AGENT 01
Input & Router
Entry Point
  • Parse keywords & entities
  • Detect urgency level
  • Classify: Medical / Legal / Dual
  • Prepare search queries
AGENT 02
Knowledge Matcher
RAG Search
  • Search medical conditions
  • Search legal case database
  • Match doctors & hospitals
  • Return consolidated results
6 curated Singapore databases
AGENT 03
Coordinator
Dual Cases Only
  • Coordinate medical + legal
  • Resolve conflicts/overlaps
  • Sequence action steps
  • Flag special requirements
Optional — activates for DUAL pathway
AGENT 04
Response Formatter
User Output
  • Prioritise by urgency
  • Surface emergency numbers
  • Structure action phases
  • Stream to frontend
